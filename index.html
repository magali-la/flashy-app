How to use Flashy:

  • Pick it: Select a deck from the sidebar to start studying.
  • Flip it: Click the card or press the Flip button to reveal the answer.
  • Stick it: Use Prev/Next to navigate through your cards and master them.

Pro tip: Use the Search bar to find specific cards, or Shuffle to randomize your study order.
